Content Marketing — systems & KPIs

Quality that scales and converts

System approach

Build a repeatable content engine: research, production, optimization, and distribution — all tied to business outcomes (leads, trials, revenue).

Playbook (executable)

  1. Audit: identify top content, gaps, and conversion leakage.
  2. Strategy: pick 1–2 pillar topics, map clusters and CTAs.
  3. Production: templates + briefs, SEO meta, schema & editorial review.
  4. Distribution: repurpose, test on social & paid, add to email nurture.
  5. Measure: organic lift, assisted conversions, lead quality and CAC movement.

Formats prioritized by ROI

Long-form research and case studies for acquisition; short-form video and social for velocity; gated reports for lead quality.
